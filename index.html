Core rules

How Eco Battle works

1. Every card uses the same five stats: Body Size, Food Chain & Energy, Habitat Adaptability, Intelligence, and Eco-Impact.

2. The combined total of all five stats must stay at or below 500.

3. Build a collection first, then choose exactly 5 cards in the War Room.

4. During a battle, only the top card is played each round, following Top Trumps logic.

5. At the end of a match, the winner can claim one rival card.
